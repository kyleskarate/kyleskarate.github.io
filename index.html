About Kyle’s Karate

Kyle’s Karate is led by experienced instructors who teach more than techniques — they teach life skills. Our mission is to help students grow physically, mentally, and emotionally through consistent, structured training.

Programs

After-School Karate

Our most popular program — weekly after-school classes at local private schools and daycares. We bring karate to your child’s campus.

Group Lessons

Small-group training for kids and families at select locations or private residences. Personalized instruction and fun energy.

Birthday Parties

Make their birthday unforgettable with a high-energy karate party — exciting drills, board breaking, and memorable fun.
