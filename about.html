About the Program

Kyle’s Karate is a traveling martial arts vendor that offers enrichment, after-school programs, birthdays, at-home private lessons, and special events in Maryland and Virginia, including areas like Potomac, McLean, Fairfax, Montgomery County, and Loudoun County.

What We Offer

  • Foundation Martial Arts Class: Taught by a team trained and supervised by Kyle, focusing on the basics, stranger-danger, bully-conflict resolution, and fostering a confident, focused attitude.
  • Birthday Parties: Customizable karate-themed parties for unforgettable fun.
  • At-Home Private Lessons: Personalized one-on-one instruction in the comfort of your home.
  • Corporate Events: Engaging and interactive experiences tailored to your needs.
  • Safe Practice: Students make contact only with the equipment, ensuring a fun and safe experience.
  • Highly Rated and Insured: Loved by students and parents alike for our fun classes and life skills teaching.
  • In-House Classes: All necessary equipment is provided, including a full uniform and white belt for newcomers.
  • Promotions and Special Events: Several belt promotions each school year, summer camps, and more.

Kyle Mitcham, Owner

Kyle Mitcham, or Coach Kyle, has been teaching martial arts since 2009, holding a black belt in traditional Tangsoodo and training in disciplines like Muay Thai. He's not just a practitioner but an innovator, having personally developed a unique style of American Karate.

Rooted in practicality, this style is not a casual invention but a carefully crafted methodology, outlined in his book. Combining traditional wisdom with modern insight, Kyle’s Karate offers an engaging and authentic experience.
