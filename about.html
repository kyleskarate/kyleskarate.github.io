About the Program

Kyle’s Karate is a traveling martial arts vendor offering enrichment, after-school programs, birthdays, at-home private lessons, and special events across Maryland and Virginia — including Potomac, McLean, Fairfax, Montgomery County, and Loudoun County.

  • Foundation Martial Arts Class: Basics, stranger-danger, bully resolution, and focus — taught by a team trained and supervised by Kyle.
  • Birthday Parties: Custom karate-themed parties for unforgettable fun.
  • At-Home Lessons: Personalized one-on-one instruction at home.
  • Corporate Events: Engaging team-building experiences.
  • Safe Practice: Contact only with equipment, not partners.
  • Highly Rated & Insured: Known for fun classes and strong life skills.
  • In-House Classes: Full uniform and white belt provided for newcomers.
  • Promotions & Events: Belt promotions, summer camps, and more throughout the year.

More on Classes

Our enrichment and afterschool programs teach focus, confidence, and respect through structured karate lessons that kids love. Every student begins with a white belt and uniform, learning essential skills safely and progressively.

Learn More About Classes

Kyle Mitcham, Owner

Kyle “Coach Kyle” Mitcham has been teaching martial arts since 2009. A Black Belt in Tang Soo Do with training in Muay Thai and other disciplines, Kyle is not just a practitioner but an innovator. He developed his own modern system of American Karate — practical, refined, and outlined in his book. Combining traditional wisdom with modern insight, Kyle’s Karate delivers an authentic and exciting experience for all ages.

Style & Philosophy

Kyle’s Karate is a unique new style of American Karate emphasizing strong technique, practicality, and mindset. The system distills the essence of traditional martial arts into clear, useful principles for modern students.
