About Kyle’s Karate

Kyle’s Karate is a traveling martial arts vendor that delivers enrichment, after-school programs, birthday parties, at-home private lessons, and events across Maryland and Virginia — including Potomac, McLean, Fairfax, and Loudoun County. :contentReference[oaicite:8]{index=8}

We emphasize real-world effectiveness, safety, and life skills. Students never spar each other; all training is done with gear under instructor control. :contentReference[oaicite:9]{index=9}

The program is led by Coach Kyle (Kyle Mitcham), who has taught since 2009 and holds a black belt in traditional Tangsoodo, supplemented by modern training and philosophy. :contentReference[oaicite:10]{index=10}

We believe in empowering students with confidence, discipline, and focus — tools they carry beyond the gym. Each year we run belt promotions, special events, and growth opportunities. :contentReference[oaicite:11]{index=11}
