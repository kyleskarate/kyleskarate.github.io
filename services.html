Programs & Services

Enrichment & Afterschool Programs

Experience a martial arts program that will engage your child as they learn the basics of kicking, punching, and blocking—all right at their school or daycare! This is a complete program, offering uniforms, belts, and tests along with top-notch instruction. No need for extra travel; your child can learn right where they are, saving you time.

Children's classes are offered at various locations, including daycares, public schools, and private institutions. Students will learn techniques and skills, earning merit awards and progressing through the Kyle’s Karate “Stripes of White” ranking system. Visible milestones mark their accomplishments.

Preschool & Kindergarten Classes

Kyle’s Karate combines exciting instruction with practical, effective techniques—perfect for developing coordination and concentration in young learners. Each student receives a new uniform and white belt on their first day. The classes include lessons on Stranger-Danger, bullying prevention, discipline, and respect. All techniques are practiced safely, never student-to-student. Belt testing reminders are sent via email a week prior, and parents and family are welcome to attend.

After-School Classes

Our afterschool karate program offers a well-rounded experience, teaching students essential martial arts skills and self-defense techniques. Discipline, respect, and self-confidence are integral to our classes, which also cover street smarts, de-escalation, and conflict resolution—all taught conveniently at your child’s school.

A Little About the Style

Kyle’s Karate is a modern martial arts class focusing on developing excellent technique and practicality. Study areas include Kicking, Punching, Blocking, Situational Awareness, and Physical and Mental Discipline. The program runs throughout the school year at various locations, offering convenience for busy parents. Kyle has also authored a book with demonstrative photographs and detailed descriptions of the techniques taught in class.
