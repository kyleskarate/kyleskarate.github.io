Enrichment & Afterschool Programs

Experience a martial arts program that will engage your child as they learn the basics of kicking, punching, and blocking—all right at their school or daycare! This complete program includes uniforms, belts, and testing, with top-notch instruction and zero commute time for parents.

Classes are offered at daycares, public schools, and private institutions. Students progress through the Kyle’s Karate “Stripes of White” ranking system, earning visible milestones and building confidence along the way.

Kyle’s Karate combines exciting instruction with practical, effective techniques—perfect for developing coordination and concentration in young learners. Each student receives a new uniform and white belt on their first day. The classes include lessons on Stranger-Danger, bullying prevention, discipline, and respect. All techniques are practiced safely, never student-to-student. Belt testing reminders are sent via email a week prior, and parents and family are welcome to attend.

Our afterschool karate program offers a well-rounded experience, teaching students essential martial arts skills and self-defense techniques. Discipline, respect, and self-confidence are integral to our classes, which also cover street smarts, de-escalation, and conflict resolution—all taught conveniently at your child’s school.

A Little About the Style

Kyle’s Karate blends modern practicality with traditional discipline. Students train in Kicking, Punching, Blocking, and Awareness, developing both physical and mental skill. The program runs year-round at multiple schools for convenience.
